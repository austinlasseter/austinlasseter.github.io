Get to know me!

Hey! It's Austin Lasseter and I'm a Software Engineer and AI/ML Instructor located in Northern Virginia. I lead remote & onsite courses in building machine learning applications. I work for ScienceLogic, a startup building the leading software product in the AIOps space. I love to create digital products at the intersection of machine learning and CI/Cd deployment, and to share with others what I've learned and built.

When I'm not throwing it down with Python, I love running marathons, kayaking, rock climbing, and playing board games with my wife and kids.
